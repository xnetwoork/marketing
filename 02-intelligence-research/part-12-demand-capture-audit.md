# Part 12: Demand Capture Audit — Existing Intent Channels

## Learning Objectives
- Understand the difference between demand capture and demand creation
- Audit your existing demand capture channels systematically
- Identify and prioritize high-intent signals you're currently missing
- Optimize conversion paths for bottom-of-funnel traffic
- Establish the right metrics to measure demand capture performance

---

## Demand Capture vs. Demand Creation

These two activities are fundamentally different and require separate strategy, budget, and measurement frameworks.

| Dimension | Demand Capture | Demand Creation |
|---|---|---|
| **Audience state** | Already problem-aware and solution-seeking | Unaware or indifferent |
| **Timing** | Now — they're ready to buy | Future — you're planting seeds |
| **Channels** | Search, review sites, branded traffic, comparison pages | Social, content, community, podcasts, events |
| **Message type** | Conversion-focused, proof-heavy | Awareness, education, narrative |
| **ROI timeline** | Immediate | 3–12 months |
| **Budget priority** | Maximize before scaling creation | Scale after capture is optimized |

**The most common B2B marketing mistake:** investing in demand creation before demand capture is optimized. If people are searching for your solution and finding a competitor, no amount of brand content will fix that.

---

## The Demand Capture Audit Framework

A demand capture audit answers: *Are we catching all the demand that already exists for what we sell?*

### Audit Step 1: Map Your Capture Channels

Document every channel where high-intent prospects could encounter you:

| Channel | Current State | Monthly Volume Estimate |
|---|---|---|
| Branded search (Google) | Rank #1 / ads running? | — |
| Category/solution search | Rank position? Conversion rate? | — |
| Competitor comparison search | Ranking for "[Competitor] alternative"? | — |
| G2 / Capterra / Trustpilot | Profile quality, review count, category rank | — |
| Direct/referral traffic | Source breakdown, conversion rate | — |
| Retargeting (website visitors) | Audience size, ad coverage | — |
| Affiliate/partner referrals | Active partnerships, traffic volume | — |

### Audit Step 2: Identify Leakage Points

For each channel, ask: *How much of the intent signal are we missing?*

- **Search:** What's our impression share? What keywords trigger competitor ads that we're not bidding on?
- **Review sites:** Are we in the right categories? Are competitors outranking us on G2/Capterra?
- **Retargeting:** What % of website visitors are we reaching with follow-up ads?
- **Branded:** Are competitors bidding on our brand name? Are we defending it?

## Intent Signal Identification

Not all traffic is equal. Demand capture is about finding and converting **high-intent signals**.

### Tier 1: Highest Intent Signals

| Signal | Example | Priority |
|---|---|---|
| Branded search | "[Your brand] pricing" | Critical |
| Competitor alternative search | "Best [Competitor] alternative" | Critical |
| Category + intent modifier | "CRM software for agencies" | High |
| Review site category browse | Top 10 lists on G2 | High |
| Direct demo/trial request | Form submission | Critical |

### Tier 2: Strong Intent Signals

- Returning visitors (2+ visits within 30 days)
- Pricing page visitors who didn't convert
- Case study or ROI calculator users
- Feature comparison page visitors
- Free trial sign-ups who haven't activated

### Tier 3: Emerging Intent Signals

- First-time blog visitors from SEO
- Email subscribers from lead magnets
- Webinar attendees
- Social followers who have visited the website

**Demand capture priority:** Always work Tier 1 to exhaustion before investing in Tier 3.

## Auditing Demand Capture by Channel

### Organic Search Audit

1. Pull your **Search Console** data — filter for queries with 100+ impressions and >3% CTR gap
2. Identify keywords where you're ranking positions 4–15 (within striking distance)
3. Look for high-value commercial-intent terms you're not appearing for at all
4. Check competitor comparison terms: "[Your brand] vs [Competitor]" — are you owning this?

**Quick wins:** Optimizing existing pages for terms you're ranking 5–15 for often doubles traffic faster than creating new content.

### Paid Search Audit

- **Branded keyword coverage:** Are you bidding on your own brand? If not, competitors may be capturing this traffic.
- **Impression share:** What % of eligible impressions are you showing for? Industry benchmark: 70%+ for priority terms.
- **Search term report:** What queries triggered your ads? Are there high-converting queries you should add as exact match?
- **Negative keyword gaps:** Are you paying for irrelevant clicks?

### Review Site Audit (G2, Capterra, Trustpilot, etc.)

| Audit Item | What to Check |
|---|---|
| Category listing | Are you in all relevant categories? |
| Review recency | Are reviews from the last 6 months? Stale profiles rank lower |
| Star rating vs. competitors | Are you within 0.2 stars of the category leader? |
| Profile completeness | All features documented, integrations listed, case studies added? |
| Review response rate | Are you responding to all reviews (especially negative)? |

**High-leverage action:** A 20-review gap vs. a competitor on G2 can mean losing 30–40% of comparison-stage traffic.

### Retargeting Coverage Audit

- **Website visitor audience size** — are you pixeling all traffic?
- **Coverage gap** — what % of website visitors are you currently reaching with retargeting ads?
- **Retargeting message relevance** — are ads personalized by the page visited (pricing vs. blog vs. case study)?
- **Frequency and recency** — are you over-serving ads to old visitors and under-serving recent high-intent ones?

## Capturing Bottom-of-Funnel Demand Efficiently

### The BOFU Content Checklist

High-intent buyers need specific content to convert. Audit whether you have:

- [ ] Comparison pages: "[Your Brand] vs [Competitor A]"
- [ ] "Best [Category] software" landing page
- [ ] Pricing page with clear tiers and FAQ
- [ ] ROI calculator or value estimator
- [ ] Case studies by industry/use case
- [ ] Demo video (under 3 minutes)
- [ ] Free trial or freemium entry point
- [ ] Objection-handling FAQ
- [ ] Social proof wall (logos, testimonials, review widgets)

Missing items in this list represent direct revenue gaps.

## Conversion Path Optimization

Getting traffic to a high-intent page is only half the job. The conversion path must match intent.

### Common Conversion Path Failures

| Problem | Symptom | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| Wrong CTA for intent level | "Download eBook" on a pricing page | Match CTA to buyer stage |
| Too much friction | 7-field form for a free trial | Reduce to email-only or Google SSO |
| No urgency or scarcity | Generic "Request a Demo" | Add specificity ("30-minute strategy call, same week") |
| Social proof missing | No logos or reviews above the fold | Add proof near the primary CTA |
| Mobile experience broken | High bounce on mobile | Prioritize mobile conversion flow |

## Demand Capture Metrics

### Primary Metrics

| Metric | Definition | Target |
|---|---|---|
| **Branded search CTR** | CTR on branded search results | >90% |
| **Impression share (paid)** | % of eligible impressions captured | >70% for priority terms |
| **Review site category rank** | Position in primary category | Top 5 |
| **BOFU conversion rate** | Demo/trial requests / BOFU page visitors | 5–15% (varies by ACV) |
| **Retargeting reach %** | % of site visitors reached with follow-up ads | >60% |

### Secondary Metrics
- Cost per demo/trial by channel
- Assisted conversion contribution by capture channel
- Review velocity (new reviews per month vs. competitors)

---

## Key Takeaways

- **Capture before you create.** Optimizing demand capture always has faster ROI than demand creation.
- **Intent signals are tiered.** Branded and comparison searches are the highest-value signals to defend.
- **Review sites are an underrated demand capture channel.** Most B2B buyers consult them before a demo.
- **Retargeting coverage gap is often the fastest fix.** Pixel everyone; follow up with relevance.
- **BOFU content gaps directly cost you revenue.** Audit and fill them before running brand campaigns.

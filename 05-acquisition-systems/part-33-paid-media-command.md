# Part 33: Paid Media Command — Meta, Google, LinkedIn & TikTok

## Learning Objectives
- Architect a multi-platform paid media strategy that drives measurable ROI
- Master campaign structure, creative strategy, and bidding on Meta, Google, LinkedIn, and TikTok
- Allocate budget intelligently across platforms based on funnel stage and business objective
- Build attribution models that connect paid spend to pipeline and revenue

---

## Why Paid Media Remains Essential

Organic channels compound slowly. Paid media gives you **speed, precision, and scale** — but only when treated as an engineered system, not a budget line item. The brands that win in paid media think architecturally: each platform plays a defined role, audiences are segmented by intent, and creative is matched to context.

---

## Paid Media Strategy Architecture

Before buying a single impression, define your **media architecture**:

| Layer | Goal | Platforms |
|---|---|---|
| Awareness | Reach new audiences at scale | Meta, TikTok, YouTube |
| Consideration | Educate and nurture | Google Display, LinkedIn, YouTube |
| Conversion | Capture high-intent demand | Google Search, Meta retargeting |
| Retention | Cross-sell, upsell, reactivate | Meta, Google |

**Rule:** Map every campaign to one layer. Mixing objectives within a campaign destroys optimization signals.

## Meta Ads: Audiences, Structure & Creative

### Campaign Structure
Use the **Campaign → Ad Set → Ad** hierarchy deliberately:
- **Campaign level:** Choose one objective (e.g., Leads, Sales, Awareness)
- **Ad set level:** Define audience, placement, schedule, and budget
- **Ad level:** Test 3–5 creative variants per ad set

### Audience Strategy
- **Cold audiences:** Broad interest targeting + Advantage+ Audience (let Meta's AI expand)
- **Lookalikes:** 1–3% LAL from high-value customers (purchase, LTV-based)
- **Retargeting:** Website visitors (30/60/90-day windows), video viewers (25%, 50%, 75%)
- **Customer lists:** Upload CRM segments for suppression and upsell campaigns

### Creative Strategy
Creative is the **primary variable** in Meta performance. Rotate:
- **UGC-style video** (15–30 seconds, hook in first 3 seconds)
- **Static carousels** for feature comparison or social proof
- **Testimonial ads** with real customer quotes and faces
- **Direct response copy:** Lead with the problem, offer the solution, end with a clear CTA

### Bidding
- Start with **Advantage+ Budget Optimization (ABO)** for testing
- Switch to **Campaign Budget Optimization (CBO)** once winning ad sets are identified
- Use **Cost Cap** for efficiency when CAC targets are firm; use **Highest Volume** for scale

## Google Ads: Search, Display, YouTube & PMAX

### Search Campaigns
- Use **exact and phrase match** for high-intent keywords
- Structure ad groups around tightly themed keyword clusters (SKAGs or STAGs)
- Write ads with keyword insertion, clear value propositions, and urgency
- Use **ad extensions**: sitelinks, callouts, structured snippets, lead forms

### Display Campaigns
- Best for retargeting and awareness, not cold conversion
- Use custom audiences (intent, in-market) + remarketing lists
- Keep creative simple: strong headline, single visual, one CTA

### YouTube Ads
- **TrueView In-Stream:** Skip after 5 seconds — hook must land in 5 seconds
- **Bumper ads (6 seconds):** Brand recall and retargeting
- Target by custom intent audience, YouTube channel, or keyword

### Performance Max (PMAX)
- Feed PMAX high-quality **asset groups**: headlines, descriptions, images, videos, logos
- Provide strong **audience signals** (customer lists, website visitors)
- Monitor search term reports; add negative keywords at the account level
- Best for: ecommerce and lead gen with strong creative assets

## LinkedIn Ads: ABM, Content Ads & Lead Gen Forms

LinkedIn is the only platform with **professional targeting at scale**. Use it for B2B.

### Targeting Levers
- Job title, seniority, function, company size, industry, skills, company name (ABM)
- **Matched Audiences:** Upload account lists, contact lists, retarget website visitors

### Ad Formats
| Format | Best Use |
|---|---|
| Sponsored Content (single image) | Awareness and thought leadership |
| Carousel Ads | Multi-step storytelling or feature showcase |
| Video Ads | Demos, testimonials, founder stories |
| Lead Gen Forms | Gated content, event registrations |
| Message Ads / Conversation Ads | Direct outreach at scale |

**Lead Gen Forms** auto-populate from LinkedIn profiles — conversion rates 2–3× higher than landing pages for cold audiences.

### Bidding
LinkedIn CPCs are expensive ($5–$15+). **Optimize ruthlessly:** narrow ICP targeting, rotate creative every 3–4 weeks, pause underperformers fast.

## TikTok Ads: Creative Requirements & Performance

TikTok rewards **native, authentic creative** — polished brand ads underperform.

### Creative Requirements
- **9:16 vertical video, 15–60 seconds**
- Hook in first 1–2 seconds (text overlay + visual action)
- Use trending audio or TikTok-native sounds
- Show product in use, not just product shots
- Creator-style content outperforms studio production by 30–50%

### Audience & Targeting
- **Interest targeting** (broad, let TikTok's algorithm optimize)
- **Custom audiences:** Website visitors, customer lists
- **Lookalike audiences:** From converters or high-value customers

### Performance Considerations
- Use **Smart Performance Campaigns** for algorithmic optimization
- TikTok's attribution window differs — compare last-click + view-through
- Strong for DTC brands targeting 18–34; growing fast in B2C services

## Cross-Platform Budget Allocation

| Business Type | Meta | Google Search | LinkedIn | TikTok | YouTube |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| SaaS B2B | 20% | 30% | 40% | 5% | 5% |
| DTC Ecommerce | 40% | 25% | 0% | 25% | 10% |
| Local Services | 30% | 55% | 0% | 10% | 5% |

**Framework:** Allocate first to demand capture (Search), then demand generation (Meta/TikTok/LinkedIn), then retention (all platforms).

## Paid Media Measurement & Attribution

Attribution is **broken by default** — use a layered approach:
1. **Last-click (platform native):** Directional, not truth
2. **MTA (multi-touch attribution):** Tools like Rockerbox, Northbeam, Triple Whale
3. **Marketing Mix Modeling (MMM):** For budgets >$500K/month
4. **Incrementality testing:** Geo holdouts, platform lift studies

**Track at minimum:** Impressions, CPM, CTR, CPC, CVR, CPA, ROAS, MER (marketing efficiency ratio)

---

## Scaling Paid Media Profitably

- **Don't scale broken creative.** Fix CVR before increasing spend.
- Scale winning ad sets by **increasing budget 20% every 48 hours**
- Expand audiences before raising bids
- Refresh creative every 3–4 weeks to fight ad fatigue
- Build a **creative testing engine:** 5+ new concepts/month minimum

---

## Key Takeaways

- **Architecture first:** Each platform and campaign has a defined funnel role
- **Creative is the lever:** On Meta and TikTok, creative quality drives 70%+ of performance
- **LinkedIn for B2B:** Expensive but unmatched professional targeting
- **PMAX and Advantage+** shift control to algorithms — feed them quality signals
- **Measure incrementally:** Platform-reported ROAS is always optimistic
- **Scale systematically:** Raise budgets gradually, refresh creative proactively

## About This Knowledge Base

This knowledge base covers how marketing actually works in practice. It is designed for:
- Founders and entrepreneurs building marketing for the first time
- Business professionals who want to understand marketing strategy
- Marketers looking to deepen their knowledge in specific areas
- Anyone curious about how companies create and grow customer relationships

Each of the 100 parts is self-contained and can be read independently, though cross-references throughout help you build a complete picture.

**What you'll find in each part:**
- Clear learning objectives
- 800–1,200 words of practical, actionable content
- Real-world examples and case studies
- Key takeaways
- Links to related parts

## How to Use This Knowledge Base

### If You're New to Marketing
Start with the Foundations section (Parts 1–8). These parts build the conceptual vocabulary and strategic mindset that every other part assumes. Then work through Strategy & Planning (Parts 9–16).

### If You're Working on a Specific Problem
Use the table of contents to jump directly to the relevant part. Every part cross-references related parts so you can navigate naturally.

### If You're Building a Marketing System
Follow the logical progression:
1. **Understand your market** (Parts 1–8)
2. **Build your strategy** (Parts 9–16)
3. **Create your content and messaging** (Parts 17–24)

# Part 02: Value Core & Outcome Mapping

## Learning Objectives
- Translate PPV platform features into customer outcomes that drive purchase
- Build a value-core statement that stays consistent across the funnel
- Create an outcome map that aligns messaging, pricing, and upsells

---

## Why Outcomes Win in PPV

PPV buyers don’t pay for a stream; they pay for an experience: exclusivity, a shared moment, professional advancement, or emotional payoff. Features like “4K streaming” only matter if they connect to the outcome the viewer wants. If your value core is feature-led, your funnel becomes price-sensitive and interchangeable.

**Outcome-first framing:**
- Feature: Multi-angle cameras → Outcome: “See the action from the angle you choose.”
- Feature: Instant replay → Outcome: “Relive the critical moments anytime.”
- Feature: Live chat → Outcome: “Be part of the crowd, even at home.”

---

## Build the Value Core Statement

Create a single sentence that anchors all funnel messaging:

**“This PPV gives [audience] the ability to [primary outcome] during [event context], with [unique proof].”**

Example: “This PPV gives MMA fans the ability to witness a championship moment live, with exclusive backstage access and guaranteed replay within 2 hours.”

The value core should be short enough to place at the top of landing pages, in subject lines, and in ad headlines without distortion.

---

## Outcome Mapping Framework

Map outcomes across three tiers:

1. **Primary Outcome (the reason they buy)**
   - Example: “Watch the main event live.”
2. **Secondary Outcomes (reinforcement)**
   - Example: “Get exclusive commentary and angle control.”
3. **Tertiary Outcomes (risk reducers)**
   - Example: “Replay access if you miss the live stream.”

For each tier, list the **proof assets** that validate the promise: trailers, testimonials, press quotes, or platform reliability stats.

---

## Align Outcomes to Funnel Stages

- **Awareness:** Show the primary outcome only.
- **Consideration:** Add secondary outcomes and proof.
- **Checkout:** Reinforce risk reducers and clarity (refunds, replay, device support).
- **Post-purchase:** Deliver the promised outcome immediately (access instructions, calendar add).

If the outcome shifts between stages, users feel baited and churn increases.

---

## Key Takeaways
- Outcomes are the real product in PPV funnels; features only support outcomes
- A single value core statement keeps the funnel consistent and persuasive
- Outcome mapping prevents the “too many features” confusion trap
- Proof assets must attach to each outcome tier for trust and conversion

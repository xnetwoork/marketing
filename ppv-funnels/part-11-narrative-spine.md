# Part 11: Narrative Spine

## Learning Objectives
- Build a narrative arc that gives the PPV event meaning and urgency
- Connect story elements to funnel conversion stages
- Avoid narrative overload while sustaining intrigue

---

## Why Narrative Matters in PPV

PPV is not just a broadcast; it is a **moment**. Narrative gives the moment stakes: rivalry, transformation, history, or scarcity. Without a story, the offer becomes a commodity and price sensitivity increases.

---

## The 4-Beat Narrative Spine

1. **Setup:** Who is involved and why it matters
2. **Tension:** The conflict, stakes, or unanswered question
3. **Turning Point:** The moment that could change everything
4. **Resolution (implied):** Only the live event reveals the outcome

This spine should appear in teasers, landing pages, and emails — but in compressed form.

---

## How to Embed Narrative in the Funnel

- **Ads/teasers:** Use setup + tension only
- **Landing page:** Add the turning point and a clear event time
- **Email series:** Expand with background and stakes
- **Event-day reminders:** Reinforce the turning point and time left

Narrative should pull people forward, not replace clarity.

---

## Key Takeaways
- Narrative turns a stream into a must-see moment
- A 4-beat spine creates tension without overcomplication
- Narrative must compress for early stages and expand in nurture
- Story supports conversion only when it ends in a clear CTA

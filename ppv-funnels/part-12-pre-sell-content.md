# Part 12: Pre-Sell Content

## Learning Objectives
- Use pre-sell content to warm audiences before the purchase moment
- Design a content sequence that increases intent without overexposure
- Balance education, emotion, and urgency in pre-sell assets

---

## The Role of Pre-Sell in PPV

Pre-sell content builds intent and reduces friction. Unlike direct-response ads, pre-sell assets are designed to **shape perception**: “This event is worth paying for.” In PPV, pre-sell is most effective when it reveals value without giving away the core experience.

---

## The 3-Asset Pre-Sell Stack

1. **Teaser asset:** 15–30 seconds, built for curiosity
2. **Context asset:** 60–120 seconds, explains stakes and why the event matters
3. **Proof asset:** testimonials, past-event highlights, or expert validation

Each asset should point to the next stage with a light CTA.

---

## Distribution Timing

- **4–6 weeks out:** Teasers to build awareness
- **2–3 weeks out:** Context assets to deepen interest
- **Final week:** Proof assets and urgency messaging

Pre-sell content should peak at the same time as ad spend, not weeks before.

---

## Key Takeaways
- Pre-sell content shapes perceived value before the ask
- A 3-asset stack prevents repetition and keeps momentum
- Timing matters more than total volume
- Pre-sell should reveal enough to excite but not enough to replace purchase

# Part 13: Lead Capture with Event-Aware Hooks

## Learning Objectives
- Capture leads without hurting immediate PPV sales
- Use event-aware incentives that feel relevant and low-friction
- Design lead magnets that increase conversion velocity

---

## Why Lead Capture Still Matters in PPV

PPV is time-bound, but not everyone buys on first exposure. Lead capture lets you convert late buyers, recover abandons, and retain future event customers. The key is to capture intent without slowing down high-intent buyers.

---

## Event-Aware Lead Hooks

Use hooks tied directly to the event:
- **Calendar reminder + local time conversion**
- **Exclusive pre-show clip** or behind-the-scenes teaser
- **Price-drop alert** (if early-bird is real)
- **Seat upgrade reminder** for premium access

Avoid generic “newsletter” capture — it doesn’t match the urgency of PPV.

---

## Placement Strategy

- **Top of funnel:** Optional lead capture after teaser (exit intent or secondary CTA)
- **Mid funnel:** Offer reminder after proof section
- **Checkout abandon:** Capture email with a “resume purchase” link

The lead capture CTA should never compete with the purchase CTA on the same line.

---

## Key Takeaways
- Lead capture is a conversion amplifier, not a primary goal
- Event-aware hooks feel useful and keep urgency intact
- Placement should be secondary to purchase flow
- Capture assets must be instantly actionable (calendar, clip, alert)

# Part 16: Retargeting Segmentation

## Learning Objectives
- Segment retargeting audiences based on PPV funnel behavior
- Deliver stage-appropriate messaging that increases return conversion
- Avoid retargeting fatigue and trust erosion

---

## Why Generic Retargeting Fails

A PPV funnel compresses time and intent. Retargeting everyone with the same ad creates fatigue and ignores the buyer’s stage. Segmentation lets you deliver the right message based on what the user already saw.

---

## Core Retargeting Segments

1. **Teaser viewers (top-of-funnel)**
   - Message: narrative + curiosity
2. **Landing engagers (mid-funnel)**
   - Message: proof + clarity + offer summary
3. **Checkout abandoners (bottom-funnel)**
   - Message: access confidence + risk reversal
4. **Past buyers (retention)**
   - Message: loyalty + early access

---

## Frequency and Timing Rules

- Limit exposure to 2–4 impressions per day per user
- Reduce frequency after purchase to avoid confusion
- Increase urgency messaging only in final 48 hours

Retargeting should feel like a helpful reminder, not a persistent chase.

---

## Key Takeaways
- Retargeting must be segmented by funnel behavior to be effective
- Messaging should evolve from narrative to proof to risk reversal
- Frequency discipline protects trust and prevents ad fatigue
- Post-purchase audiences need different messaging or suppression

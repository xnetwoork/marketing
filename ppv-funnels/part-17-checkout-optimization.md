# Part 17: Checkout Optimization

## Learning Objectives
- Reduce PPV checkout friction and improve completion rates
- Identify the highest-impact checkout elements for PPV funnels
- Design a checkout flow that balances speed and trust

---

## The PPV Checkout Reality

The buyer is already sold. Checkout drop-off happens because of **friction or doubt**, not lack of desire. Every extra field or unclear policy is a revenue leak.

---

## The 5 Highest-Leverage Checkout Fixes

1. **Shorten the form** — email + payment is enough for most PPV events
2. **Add trusted payment options** — credit card + digital wallets
3. **Reconfirm event details** — time, date, device support
4. **Surface refund and replay policy** — reduce perceived risk
5. **Enable guest checkout** — account creation after purchase

---

## Visual and Copy Checklist

- Single-column layout for mobile
- One dominant CTA with clear action text
- Security indicators placed near payment fields
- Remove navigation links that distract

Every element should either increase trust or speed.

---

## Key Takeaways
- Checkout optimization is mostly about removing doubt and friction
- Reconfirming event details reduces refunds and support volume
- Guest checkout and wallet options materially increase completion rates
- Checkout content should be minimal but confidence-building

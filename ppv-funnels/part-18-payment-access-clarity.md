# Part 18: Payment & Access Clarity

## Learning Objectives
- Communicate PPV access steps with zero ambiguity
- Reduce refund and support requests by setting access expectations
- Align payment confirmation with immediate value delivery

---

## Access Clarity Is Conversion Insurance

PPV buyers are risk-averse because access failures are common. If they’re unsure how they will watch, they hesitate or refund. Access clarity must be explicit, visual, and repeated.

---

## The 3-Step Access Script

Include this on landing pages, checkout, and confirmation:
1. **Purchase access** (one-time payment)
2. **Receive your access link** (email + SMS)
3. **Watch live or on replay** (device list + support contact)

This script should be visible in a single screen on mobile.

---

## Payment Confirmation That Delivers Value

Immediately after purchase:
- Display event time with timezone conversion
- Provide a direct “Watch” link or calendar add
- Reconfirm replay policy and support contact

The confirmation page is part of the product experience, not just a receipt.

---

## Key Takeaways
- Access clarity reduces hesitation and protects against refunds
- A 3-step access script should appear across the funnel
- The confirmation page must deliver immediate value cues
- Clear device and timing details build trust at the most sensitive moment

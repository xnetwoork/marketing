# Part 19: Trust Proof Stack

## Learning Objectives
- Build a layered proof system that reduces PPV buyer anxiety
- Place proof assets at the right moments in the funnel
- Distinguish between social proof, technical proof, and narrative proof

---

## Why PPV Needs a Proof Stack

PPV is a high-risk purchase in the buyer’s mind: “Will it stream? Will it be worth it?” A single testimonial is not enough. You need a **stack** of proof types that answer different doubts.

---

## The 4 Proof Layers

1. **Social proof** — testimonials, ratings, community counts
2. **Technical proof** — uptime stats, streaming reliability, device compatibility
3. **Event proof** — past event highlights, press coverage
4. **Authority proof** — endorsements, notable partners or sponsors

Each layer handles a different objection, and together they create confidence.

---

## Proof Placement Guide

- **Landing mid-section:** Social + event proof
- **Offer section:** Authority proof
- **Checkout:** Technical proof and reliability assurance

If proof is only at the bottom of the page, it does not influence decision timing.

---

## Key Takeaways
- A multi-layer proof stack addresses multiple types of buyer doubt
- Technical proof is especially important in PPV where access failures are common
- Proof placement should align with stage-specific objections
- Consistent proof across ads, landing, and checkout strengthens conversion

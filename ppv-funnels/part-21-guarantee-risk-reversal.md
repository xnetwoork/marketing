# Part 21: Guarantee & Risk Reversal

## Learning Objectives
- Design PPV guarantees that increase trust without overexposing risk
- Communicate refund and support policies with clarity
- Use risk reversal as a conversion lever, not an afterthought

---

## Why Risk Reversal Matters in PPV

PPV buyers worry about two things: **access failures** and **value regret**. A strong guarantee reduces both. But guarantees only work when they are clear, enforceable, and easy to understand.

---

## Types of PPV Guarantees

1. **Access Guarantee** — full refund if the stream fails to start
2. **Playback Guarantee** — replay provided within a defined window
3. **Quality Guarantee** — support response within X minutes

Use only guarantees you can operationally deliver.

---

## Guarantee Placement and Language

- Place a short guarantee statement near the primary CTA
- Provide full terms in a dedicated FAQ or modal
- Avoid vague language (“We’ll make it right”) in favor of clear rules

Clarity beats generosity when it comes to trust.

---

## Key Takeaways
- Guarantees reduce PPV purchase anxiety and boost conversion
- Access and replay guarantees are the most effective in this category
- Guarantee language must be clear, specific, and operationally feasible
- Risk reversal should appear before checkout, not only after purchase

# Part 24: Post-Purchase Engagement

## Learning Objectives
- Design post-purchase experiences that reduce refunds and increase satisfaction
- Deliver immediate value reinforcement after the PPV purchase
- Prepare the audience for replay and future event offers

---

## The First 10 Minutes After Purchase

Post-purchase engagement is where buyer confidence is either reinforced or lost. The goal is to immediately confirm they made the right decision.

**Immediate elements:**
- Access link and event time
- Device support list
- Replay policy
- Support contact with response promise

---

## Reinforcement Content

Send a short “what to expect” message:
- Schedule highlights
- Warm-up content (interviews, backstage)
- How to join chat or community features

This increases anticipation and reduces drop-off at event time.

---

## Key Takeaways
- Post-purchase engagement reduces refunds by validating the decision
- Immediate access clarity is the most important reinforcement
- Warm-up content increases attendance and satisfaction
- The confirmation moment sets the tone for replay and future offers

# Part 25: In-Stream Upsells

## Learning Objectives
- Introduce upsells during the live experience without harming satisfaction
- Identify upsell moments that feel natural and valuable
- Design upgrade paths that increase ARPU in PPV platforms

---

## Upsells Should Enhance, Not Interrupt

In-stream upsells work only when they improve the experience the viewer is already enjoying. Interruptions or aggressive popups reduce satisfaction and increase churn.

---

## High-Value Upsell Moments

- **Pre-show window:** Offer premium angles or backstage feed
- **Intermission:** Promote replay extension or bonus content
- **Post-event:** Offer VIP Q&A or extended highlights

Keep upsells contextual: “Enhance this experience” works better than “buy more.”

---

## Upgrade Design Rules

- Single click upgrade
- Immediate access after purchase
- Clear differentiation from the core experience
- Optional, non-blocking UI placement

If upgrading introduces friction, revenue gain disappears.

---

## Key Takeaways
- In-stream upsells must feel additive, not disruptive
- The best upsell moments are natural breaks in the experience
- One-click upgrades with immediate access maximize conversion
- Poorly timed upsells can damage satisfaction and retention

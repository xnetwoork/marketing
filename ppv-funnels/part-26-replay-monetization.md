# Part 26: Replay Monetization

## Learning Objectives
- Build replay offers that extend revenue after the live event
- Position replays as a second experience, not a leftover
- Structure replay windows and pricing to maximize conversion

---

## The Replay Window Is a Second Funnel

Replay buyers are often different from live buyers: time-poor, price-sensitive, and more focused on outcomes than experience. Treat replays as a secondary product with its own positioning.

---

## Replay Offer Options

- **Short window replay:** 48–72 hours, priced lower than live
- **Premium replay bundle:** live + extended replay + bonus content
- **Evergreen replay:** for educational events or conferences

Your replay window should align with the content’s shelf life.

---

## Messaging for Replay Buyers

- Emphasize flexibility and convenience
- Highlight key moments and outcomes
- Clearly state availability and expiration

Replay messaging should not feel like a “missed out” penalty; it should feel like a smart choice.

---

## Key Takeaways
- Replay monetization is a separate funnel stage with unique buyers
- Different replay windows match different content types
- Replay messaging should emphasize flexibility and outcomes
- Clear replay policies reduce confusion and support load

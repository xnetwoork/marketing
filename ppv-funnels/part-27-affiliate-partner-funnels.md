# Part 27: Affiliate & Partner Funnels

## Learning Objectives
- Structure partner funnels that expand PPV distribution without message drift
- Provide affiliates with assets that preserve brand and conversion quality
- Track partner performance with clean attribution

---

## Why Partner Funnels Matter

Partners can drive high-intent traffic quickly, but only if messaging is aligned. Uncontrolled partner copy or misleading promotions can increase refunds and damage brand trust.

---

## Partner Funnel Requirements

- **Unique landing pages** matched to partner audience
- **Pre-approved messaging kit** (headline, CTA, and value core)
- **Clear incentive structure** (rev share, tiered bonuses)
- **Tracking links** with clean UTM structures

Provide partners with a short “why this event matters” script to keep narrative consistency.

---

## Partner Performance Controls

- Monitor conversion rates per partner weekly
- Require proof of claims before approval
- Pause underperforming partners quickly to prevent wasted traffic

---

## Key Takeaways
- Partners can scale PPV funnels fast, but only with strict message alignment
- Unique landing pages prevent audience mismatch and increase conversion
- Clear incentives and tracking make partner relationships sustainable
- Quality control protects your core brand and refund rates

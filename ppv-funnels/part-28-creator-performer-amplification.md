# Part 28: Creator & Performer Amplification

## Learning Objectives
- Use creators or performers to amplify PPV demand without diluting the brand
- Provide assets that align with creator voice and funnel messaging
- Convert creator-driven traffic with trust-preserving landing pages

---

## Creators Are Trust Channels

For PPV events, the performer or creator often carries more trust than the platform. Harness that trust with aligned messaging and clear calls to action.

---

## The Creator Amplification Kit

Provide a lightweight kit:
- 2–3 approved talking points tied to the value core
- Short teaser clips optimized for each platform
- CTA copy that points to a clean landing page
- Unique tracking links

Let creators adapt the tone, but anchor the promise.

---

## Conversion Considerations

Creator traffic tends to be high intent but **high expectation**. Reinforce:
- Event timing and access steps
- Device compatibility
- Guarantees and replay policy

Meeting expectations prevents creator-driven refunds and backlash.

---

## Key Takeaways
- Creator amplification is a trust multiplier when messaging is aligned
- Lightweight kits preserve authenticity while protecting the value core
- Creator traffic needs strong access clarity to avoid disappointment
- Tracking links are essential for understanding partner ROI

# Part 33: Attribution & Measurement

## Learning Objectives
- Build a measurement system that captures PPV funnel performance end-to-end
- Combine platform analytics with direct feedback to improve attribution accuracy
- Use measurement insights to reallocate spend during short PPV cycles

---

## The Attribution Challenge in PPV

PPV funnels compress time and span multiple devices. Buyers may see a social ad on mobile, search on desktop, and purchase on a TV-linked device. Traditional last-click models undercount top-of-funnel influence.

---

## The Minimum Viable Measurement Stack

- **UTM discipline** across all paid and partner sources
- **Server-side conversion tracking** to reduce browser signal loss
- **Post-purchase survey** (“Where did you hear about this event?”)
- **Event timeline dashboard** (hourly conversion + traffic peaks)

This stack provides directional accuracy without heavy infrastructure.

---

## Rapid Optimization Loop

- Review hourly performance in the final 48 hours
- Shift spend toward sources with lower CPA and higher completion rate
- Monitor refund and support tickets as a quality signal

Measurement should influence decisions within the same event cycle, not after.

---

## Key Takeaways
- PPV attribution requires multiple signals due to multi-device behavior
- UTM discipline and server-side tracking are the core foundations
- Post-purchase surveys fill gaps that analytics miss
- Measurement must drive real-time budget shifts before the event ends

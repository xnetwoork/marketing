# Part 34: Churn & Refund Analysis

## Learning Objectives
- Identify the root causes of PPV refunds and churn
- Use refund patterns to improve funnel messaging and delivery
- Reduce future churn by addressing access and expectation failures

---

## Refunds Are a Signal, Not Just a Cost

Every refund is a data point. PPV refunds usually fall into three categories: access failure, expectation mismatch, or buyer remorse. Treat refunds as a funnel feedback loop.

---

## The Refund Diagnostic Framework

1. **Access failure** — logins, device compatibility, stream stability
2. **Expectation mismatch** — overpromised features or unclear event format
3. **Timing conflict** — buyers who misread the event time

Track refund reasons with a simple tag system in support tickets.

---

## Funnel Fixes Based on Refund Causes

- Access failure → improve device clarity and support visibility
- Expectation mismatch → tighten promise hierarchy and proofs
- Timing conflict → add local time conversion in more locations

Refund analysis should immediately influence copy and operational changes.

---

## Key Takeaways
- Refunds are a feedback loop for funnel and operational fixes
- Most PPV refunds come from access issues and expectation mismatch
- Simple tagging of refund reasons uncovers high-leverage fixes
- Reducing refunds improves revenue and long-term brand equity

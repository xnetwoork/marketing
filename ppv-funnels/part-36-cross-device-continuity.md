# Part 36: Cross-Device Continuity

## Learning Objectives
- Design a PPV funnel that supports multi-device journeys
- Reduce friction between purchase device and viewing device
- Use continuity features to increase conversion and attendance

---

## The PPV Device Reality

Many users discover the event on mobile, purchase on desktop, and watch on TV. If the funnel assumes a single device, you introduce confusion and drop-offs.

---

## Continuity Features That Matter

- **Email magic link** for instant login
- **“Send to TV” access option** on confirmation
- **Persistent cart** across devices
- **Device setup guide** before event start

These reduce cognitive load and make the purchase feel safe.

---

## Messaging for Multi-Device Journeys

- “Buy on your phone, watch on your TV” should be explicit
- Include device compatibility icons throughout the funnel
- Offer a quick “test your device” link before the event

Clarity is a conversion lever in multi-device flows.

---

## Key Takeaways
- Cross-device continuity is essential in PPV funnels
- Magic links and device transfer options reduce drop-off
- Explicit messaging about device flexibility builds confidence
- Testing device readiness lowers support volume on event day

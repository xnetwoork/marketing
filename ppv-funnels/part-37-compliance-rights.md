# Part 37: Compliance & Rights

## Learning Objectives
- Identify the compliance and licensing factors that affect PPV funnels
- Communicate regional availability and usage rights clearly
- Reduce legal and refund risks through transparent policies

---

## Compliance Is Part of the Funnel

PPV events often have regional rights restrictions, content usage rules, and consumer protection requirements. If these constraints are not communicated clearly, the funnel will convert the wrong users and generate refunds.

---

## Core Compliance Areas

- **Regional availability** — explicit geo-restrictions
- **Age restrictions** — if required by content type
- **Usage rights** — personal viewing vs. commercial use
- **Refund and chargeback policies** — aligned with local regulations

These need to be visible before checkout, not buried in terms.

---

## Communication Best Practices

- Use a clear “Available in” section with country list or region map
- Include a short “Usage rights” note near the offer
- Link to full terms without hiding key restrictions

Clarity prevents post-purchase disputes and reduces support load.

---

## Key Takeaways
- Compliance affects conversion quality as much as it affects legal risk
- Regional and usage restrictions must be visible before purchase
- Transparent policies reduce refunds and chargebacks
- Compliance messaging should be simple, not legalistic
